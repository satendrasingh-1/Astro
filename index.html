Our Story

and the Personal

Celestia Astrology was founded on a single conviction: that the cosmos speaks, and every person deserves to hear what it says about them.

15+ Years of Practice
Meet the Astrologer

Ariana Celestine

Ariana Celestine is a certified Vedic and Western astrologer with over fifteen years of dedicated practice spanning four continents. Her journey began in the ancient libraries of Varanasi, where she studied under Pandit Rajan Mishra, and continued through formal training in modern psychological astrology at the London School of Astrology.

Her approach is never mechanical. Ariana believes that a birth chart is not a fixed destiny but a living map — one that shows you your most authentic nature and the rhythms through which life invites you to grow.

Philosophy

Ariana practices astrology as a compassionate language — not prediction, but profound self-understanding. Her readings integrate Jyotish precision, Jungian depth psychology, and genuine human warmth to create an experience that is both illuminating and grounding.

Training & Credentials

Certified by the British Association for Vedic Astrology and the International Society for Astrological Research (ISAR), Ariana has delivered over 3,200 individual consultations and has been featured in Vogue India, The Guardian, and Watkins Mind Body Spirit magazine.

What Drives Us

Mission, Vision & Values

Mission

To make the wisdom of the cosmos accessible, compassionate, and genuinely useful — helping people move through life's pivotal moments with clarity, confidence, and courage.

Vision

A world where people understand the rhythms of their own lives deeply enough to make choices that are authentic, intentional, and aligned with their highest potential.

Values

Honesty above flattery. Depth over breadth. Compassion without sentimentality. We tell you what the stars genuinely indicate — not what you simply want to hear.

🌠

Why Our Clients Trust Us

Every client receives Ariana's full attention and expertise — no assistants, no automated readings. Our practice is grounded in discretion, accuracy, and genuine care. We do not traffic in fear or vague generalities. We offer you real insight, respectfully delivered, so that you leave each session feeling informed, empowered, and at peace.
